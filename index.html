Kenya Wildlife.

In Kenya there are several wildlife animals living in the game reserves and others at gamepark.They are taken care of by the Kenya Wildlife Service officers.Whenever the country gets an opportunity to be visited by tourists from all the conners of the world,the tour guides in corporation with the KWS officers take the tourist around the park or reserve so as toreduce chances of endangering the tourist from dangerous wild animals.
